Peer-reviewed journal article based on scientific research on Environmental Microplastic Pollution

Published: August 2021

Link to Journal

Aided in feminist and anticolonial research at the Civic Laboratory for Environmental Action Research (CLEAR lab) at Memorial University of Newfoundland that specializes in monitoring plastic pollution. Published a peer-reviewed article on Elsevier. The study aimed to assess the abundance and types of plastic pollution in the surface waters of the Eastern Arctic, specifically within Inuit Nunangat. It sought to understand the sources and distribution of plastic debris in this region. Additionally, the research emphasized the importance of integrating Indigenous knowledge with scientific methods to enhance environmental monitoring and stewardship.
